HOW TO READ IT

Each column is one digit of HH:MM:SS in binary-coded decimal. Rows are worth 8, 4, 2, 1 (top to bottom). Add the values of the lit LEDs in a column to get that digit.
example: LEDs at 4 and 1 lit → 4+1 = 5.
dashed LEDs can never light for that digit.
columns: H H · M M · S S — live digits shown under each column.
