How It Works

1
Choose a source
Pick your whole screen, a single window, or one browser tab. Your browser shows its own secure picker — this tool only suggests a preference.
2
Add audio (optional)
Toggle the microphone and/or system audio. When both are on they are mixed together with the Web Audio API into a single track.
3
Record, pause, resume
A live timer and blinking dot show you're rolling. Pause and resume as many times as you like — the clip stays continuous.
4
Download
Stop to finish. The clip is saved as MP4 where your browser supports it, otherwise WebM — either plays everywhere and is 100% yours.
